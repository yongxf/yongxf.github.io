Yongxiang (Bruce) Fan

 

Welcome to my website!

I am the Co-founder and Chief Technology Officer of Anyware Robotics Inc., a company I co-founded in 2023. We develop multi-purpose robotic systems for warehouse automation and covers a few applications such as truck unloading, palletization, depalletization and case picking.

Prior to founding Anyware, I was a Senior Research Scientist at the FANUC Advanced Research Lab (2019–2022), where I worked on AI-based grasping and motion planning technologies, reporting directly to FANUC's executive leadership.

I earned my Ph.D. in Robotics from the University of California, Berkeley, where I conducted research in the Mechanical Systems Control (MSC) Laboratory under the supervision of Prof. Masayoshi Tomizuka.

My research interests lie at the intersection of robotics, artificial intelligence, and control theory, with a focus on robotic dexterity (grasping, manipulation, and assembly) and human–robot interaction (collision avoidance, motion planning, and collaborative autonomy).
